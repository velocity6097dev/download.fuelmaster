Everything You Need

Engineered for maximum forecourt efficiency and zero revenue leakage.

1. Automated Density Engine

Instantly calculate ASTM 53B standard density at 15°C with zero margin for error. Simply input your observed hydrometer reading and current temperature. FuelMaster's core engine bypasses the need for manual, physical calibration books, ensuring your daily morning density checks are 100% OMC-compliant and recorded in under 5 seconds.

2. Dip to Volume Converter

Stop estimating your inventory. Convert physical underground tank dips (in centimeters or millimeters) directly into highly accurate liter volumes. Our proprietary algorithm maps your exact tank calibration charts, instantly calculating current stock levels for MS, HSD, and XP95 without flipping through hundreds of paper pages.

3. Tanker Variance Audit

Protect your dealership from transit losses. Audit incoming Tank Trucks (TT) by entering your decantation density against the supply location's invoice (Challan) density. The system calculates temperature-adjusted volumetric variations, automatically flagging the load as ACCEPTABLE or warning you to REJECT it if it crosses statutory limits.

4. Manager Action Workflows

Enforce strict operational accountability across all shifts. Your forecourt managers receive automated daily checklists requiring them to log physical dips, complete shift-change totalizer readings, verify safety equipment, and declare banking deposits, ensuring critical standard operating procedures (SOPs) are never skipped.

5. W&M Stamping Invoice Maker

Navigate annual inspections effortlessly. Generate 100% compliant Weights & Measures (W&M) stamping invoices on the fly. Enter standard nozzle details, and the app instantly auto-populates dealership headers, generating an inspector-ready, highly accurate PDF document ready for print or official digital submission.

6. Secure Sub-User Profiles

Maintain ultimate administrative authority. As the owner, create individual, restricted-access accounts for your managers and staff. Track exactly who logged which density reading, while ensuring your core station configurations, tank charts, and sensitive financial logs remain completely secure behind your master lock.

7. Real-Time Cloud Sync

Experience seamless, interconnected operations. Every dip calculation, density check, and to-do list completion instantly syncs via our secure backend to all registered devices at your station. Monitor your forecourt's live status remotely, with automatic data backups ensuring you never lose historical logs.

8. OMC Custom Branding Engine

Personalize your digital workspace. The FuelMaster OS allows you to instantly switch the entire application interface to match your specific Oil Marketing Company's official brand guidelines. Features fully optimized, high-contrast Day and Night shift modes for perfect visibility on the forecourt, regardless of the time or weather.
